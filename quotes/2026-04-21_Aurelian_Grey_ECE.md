quote: “Until you make the unconscious conscious, it will direct your life and you will call it fate.”
author: Carl Jung
what_it_means_to_me: We often believe we are in control thinking clearly, acting with maturity, and understanding ourselves. But much of what drives us comes from parts of the mind we don’t even see. We mistake hidden patterns, fears, and unresolved thoughts for “who we are.” This quote wakes me up to the fact that unless I confront what lies beneath, I am not truly in control, I am just unaware.

source: Psychological writings of Carl Jung
