---
quote: “Even a brick wants to be something.”
author: Louis Kahn

what_it_means_to_me:
"A brick is never just a brick. It has the potential to become a weapon, a path, a shelter, or part of something extraordinary. What it becomes is shaped by how it is used.
We are no different. Our mind holds the same raw potential capable of building, creating, and rising beyond limits. The difference lies in intention and choice.
We shouldn’t drift into becoming something by chance. Like that brick, we must decide what we want to become and shape ourselves with purpose until we get there."
